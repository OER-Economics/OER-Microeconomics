Open vs. Closed: Play a Season

In a closed league the same teams return every year. In an open league the bottom clubs drop out and hungry challengers rise up. Run a few seasons and watch how differently membership behaves.

Try it: pick a structure, then hit Play season a few times. In closed mode the table's cast never changes. In open mode, finish last and you're gone — replaced by a promoted club. That churn is the heart of the European model.
