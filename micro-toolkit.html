Welfare & Policy

Surplus, Taxes & Deadweight Loss

A per-unit tax drives a wedge between the price buyers pay and the price sellers keep. It raises revenue but shrinks the quantity traded — and the lost gains from trade are the deadweight loss. Who bears the tax depends on the slopes.
