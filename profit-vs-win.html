Buying Wins: Two Owners, Two Stopping Points

A team can buy wins — but each win costs more and earns less than the last. Drag how many wins the owner buys and watch where a profit maximizer and a win maximizer choose to stop.

Try it: slide up to the profit-max point (widest TR–TC gap), then keep going to the win-max point (where TR meets TC and profit hits zero). Switch markets to see the big-market team buy more of everything.
